## Overview

Saved Ads lets you bookmark high-value ads from the feed for later analysis. Every saved ad is stored in your personal collection and can be organized into projects.

## Saving Ads

There are two ways to save an ad:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quick Save from Feed">
    Click the bookmark icon in the ad row (table view) or card corner (grid view). The icon fills to indicate saved status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save from Ad Modal">
    Open any ad's detail modal and click **Save Ad** or **Manage Save** to add it to your collection and assign projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Saved ads sync instantly across devices. Your collection is stored securely in the database and tied to your account.
</Info>

## Viewing Saved Ads

Access your saved collection:

1. Click the **Saved** tab at the top of the dashboard
2. The feed switches to show only your bookmarked ads
3. All filters and sorting options remain available
4. The count updates to reflect your saved collection size

### Saved Feed Features

- **Same interface** — Table/grid view works identically to All Ads
- **Independent filters** — Saved scope maintains separate filter state
- **Project assignment** — Organize saved ads into folders
- **Bulk actions** — Quickly manage multiple saved items

## Managing Saved Ads

### Unsaving Ads

Remove ads from your collection:

<Steps>
  <Step title="From Feed">
    Click the filled bookmark icon to toggle off
  </Step>
  <Step title="From Ad Modal">
    Open the ad and click **Manage Save**, then deselect all projects and close
  </Step>
  <Step title="From Projects View">
    Use the trash icon to remove from specific projects or entire collection
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Unsaving an ad removes it from ALL projects. If you only want to remove it from one project, use the project-specific remove action instead.
</Warning>

### Viewing Save Status

Saved ads show visual indicators:

- **Filled bookmark icon** — Ad is in your collection
- **Project count badge** — Number of projects containing this ad (shown in modal)
- **"Saved" label** — Appears in ad modal landing page section

## Project Assignment

Every saved ad can belong to one or more projects:

### Assigning Projects

1. Click **Save Ad** or **Manage Save** in the ad modal
2. A project selection modal appears
3. Check the boxes for projects to include this ad
4. Click **Save** to confirm

### Viewing Project Membership

When viewing a saved ad:

- The modal shows "Saved · X projects" in the landing page card
- The number updates as you add/remove project associations
- Projects are listed in the save modal with checkboxes

<Note>
  An ad must be saved before you can assign it to projects. Unsaving removes all project associations.
</Note>

## Saved Collection Workflow

Typical workflow for ad research:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse & Bookmark">
    Scan the All Ads feed and quick-save interesting creatives
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Collection">
    Switch to Saved tab to review your bookmarks
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize into Projects">
    Open ads and assign them to relevant project folders
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by Project">
    Use Projects tab to view ads grouped by campaign or niche
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Undo Actions

AdRecon provides a 5-second undo window for save/unsave actions:

- A toast notification appears with an **Undo** button
- Click to reverse the action before it commits
- After 5 seconds, the change persists to the database

<Info>
  Pending actions show a spinner on the bookmark icon. You can queue multiple actions — they'll process sequentially.
</Info>

## Limits & Quotas

Currently, there are no hard limits on:

- Number of saved ads per account
- Number of projects an ad can belong to
- Saved collection size
